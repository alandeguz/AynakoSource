My parents and the upcoming Christmas

The Christmas holiday is coming up soon. Making plans to see everyone. It makes me nostalgic for years past. Somedays I wish my son was still a little kid, bouncing around the tree and yammering about his guesses as to what's inside all the presents 😍🎄.

I'm so lucky to still have my parents in my life. As my son goes through different stages of adulthood, I reflect on my role as a dad, and how the relationships with my parents have evolved over the years. We all strive to be as good to our kids as our parents were to us.

Love you, Mom and Dad!!
