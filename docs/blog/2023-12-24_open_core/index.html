MacOS Sonoma on my Macbook Pro 2016

Many thanks to the folks at Dortania for developing the OpenCore Legacy Patcher. I've taken my 2016 Macbook Pro and upgraded it MacOS Sonoma 14.2.1. It was originally running Monterey 12.7, which was the last officially supported version. Saving the environment... and my pocketbook.
